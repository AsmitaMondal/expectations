The Expectations'24 Fest has chosen the PANDA as its official mascot to symbolize the theme "Data Sparks," which promotes the convergence of AI and sustainability. Once classified as endangered, the panda now stands as a vulnerable species, reflecting a significant improvement in its population due to effective sustainable practices in both nature and technology. By adopting the panda as a mascot, the fest aims to highlight the positive impact of responsible tech usage and advocate for AI applications that foster sustainability and planetary progress. This choice not only resonates with the values of the event but also inspires participants to embrace innovative solutions for a healthier planet.
