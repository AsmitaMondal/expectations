Event Description:

Unleash your creativity in this exciting pitching competition. In Round 1, each team member delivers a 3-minute pitch on a business idea, followed by rapid Q&A. The best 4-5 teams advance to the Final Round, where they’ll have 10 minutes to prepare a pitch on a surprise topic. Judging focuses on originality, clarity, confidence, and Q&A handling. Stay sharp—time overruns or last-minute changes will cost points. Don’t forget your laptop.


Rules:

- Teams must contain 2-3 members.
- Each pitch must be completed within 3 minutes, with mandatory participation from every team member.
- Finalists get 10 minutes to prepare a pitch on an on-spot topic.
- PPTs must be submitted immediately after the preparation time ends.
- Any changes to slides after submission will result in penalties.
