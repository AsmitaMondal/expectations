Event Description:

Data Feud is an exhilarating team-based competition that tests your knowledge of data science, machine learning, and general trivia! With two rounds of fast-paced fun—Survey Says and Face-off—prepare to challenge your data skills and intuition.


Rules:

- Each team must have 3 participants.
- Teams will take turns guessing popular answers from survey data in Round 1 where each team will have 3 chances (strikes) before their turn is over.
- In the Face-off, one member from each team will answer rapid-fire questions.
- Points are awarded based on their accuracy, speed, and ranks/weightage of their answer.
- Teams must bring their own devices for buzzer responses.
