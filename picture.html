Event Description:

The event combines creativity, quick thinking, and knowledge of Visualization, Statistics, and Data Science. Teams will engage in drawing, guessing, storytelling, and data application challenges.


Rules:

- 2 members per team.
- Teams pick a word from Visualization, Statistics, or Data Science; one member draws, the other guesses (60 seconds per word, 5 points per correct guess).
- Top 50% of teams proceed to the next round.
- Teams explain how to apply the statistical and data science measures guessed during rapid-fire to a provided dataset (judged on clarity and reasoning, 50 points).
